Oats

Milk oats, also known as oatmeal or oat porridge, is a popular breakfast dish made by cooking rolled or steel-cut oats in milk (or water) until they become soft and creamy.

Paneer

Paneer is a fresh, unsalted cheese commonly used in Indian cuisine. It is made by curdling milk with an acidic substance, such as lemon juice or vinegar

Curd: Creamy, tangy, probiotic-rich dairy product used in cooking, dressings, and desserts; essential in Indian cuisine; versatile, healthy, and delicious.
